KARACHI BITES — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Is your food halal?
A: Yes, 100%. All our meat is halal and sourced from certified suppliers. We can provide halal certificates on request.

Q: Do you have vegetarian options?
A: Yes. Our vegetarian options include Daal Makhni, Palak Paneer, Dahi Baray, Aloo Tikki Chaat, and plain/garlic naan. About 20% of our menu is vegetarian-friendly.

Q: Can I customize my order? For example, less spicy or no onions?
A: Absolutely. Just mention your preferences when placing the order. We can adjust spice levels (mild, medium, or extra spicy) and accommodate most ingredient requests. Note that some dishes like biryani are cooked in bulk so customization is limited for those.

Q: Do you have outdoor seating?
A: Yes, we have a small outdoor seating area with 6 tables. It is available on a first-come basis and cannot be reserved. Outdoor seating is closed during heavy rain.

Q: Is there parking available?
A: We don't have our own parking lot, but Jinnah Super's main parking plaza is directly opposite our shop. It's a 1-minute walk. Parking charges are Rs. 30/hour (managed by the market association, not by us).

Q: Do you offer deals or discounts?
A: Yes. We run weekly deals on our Instagram page every Monday. We also offer a 10% discount for students with a valid university ID (dine-in only). During Ramadan, we have special iftar and sehri deals.

Q: Can I place a bulk order for my office?
A: Yes. For orders above Rs. 5,000 we offer a 5% bulk discount. For orders above Rs. 15,000 please use our catering service. Contact us on WhatsApp at 0311-5551234 for bulk/corporate orders.

Q: Do you sell frozen or ready-to-cook items?
A: Not currently. Everything we serve is prepared fresh. We have considered launching a frozen kabab line but it is not available yet.

Q: What is your busiest time?
A: Weekday evenings from 8 PM to 10 PM and all of Friday/Saturday nights. If you're dining in during peak hours, we strongly recommend making a reservation.

Q: Can I book the restaurant for a private event?
A: Our private dining room accommodates 10-20 guests with a minimum spend of Rs. 5,000. For full restaurant bookings (50+ guests), contact Ahmed Raza at 0311-5551234 at least one week in advance. Full restaurant bookings are only available on weekday afternoons (Monday-Thursday, 2 PM - 6 PM).

Q: Do you offer any loyalty program?
A: Yes. We have a stamp card — buy 10 biryanis and get the 11th free. Ask for a stamp card at the counter next time you visit. The loyalty program is for dine-in and takeaway only, not delivery.

Q: Are you on FoodPanda or other delivery apps?
A: No. We handle all deliveries ourselves to maintain food quality and keep prices low. You can order via phone call (051-2345678) or WhatsApp (0311-5551234).
