KARACHI BITES — DELIVERY & STORE POLICIES

=== DELIVERY ===
We deliver within a 7 km radius of our main branch in F-7 Markaz, Islamabad.
Delivery is available from 12:00 PM to 11:30 PM daily.
Minimum order for delivery is Rs. 500.
Delivery charge is Rs. 100 for orders under Rs. 1500. Free delivery on orders above Rs. 1500.
Estimated delivery time is 30-45 minutes depending on distance and order volume.
During rain or extreme weather, delivery times may increase to 60-90 minutes.
We deliver through our own riders. We are NOT available on FoodPanda or Cheetay.

=== PAYMENT ===
We accept cash on delivery (COD).
We accept JazzCash and EasyPaisa for online payment. Send payment to 0311-5551234 and share screenshot with your order.
We accept bank transfer to HBL account (details provided on request).
Credit/debit card payment is available for dine-in only via POS machine.
We do not accept credit cards for delivery orders.

=== REFUND & COMPLAINTS ===
If your order is wrong or missing items, contact us within 30 minutes of delivery.
Call our complaint line at 051-2345678 or WhatsApp 0311-5551234.
We will either replace the item or issue a full refund for the incorrect/missing item.
Refunds are processed within 24 hours to your original payment method.
We do not offer refunds for change of mind or if you simply did not like the taste.
For hygiene and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns of food that has been partially consumed.

=== CATERING ===
We offer catering for events and parties with a minimum order of Rs. 15,000.
Catering menu is different from our regular menu — contact us for the catering brochure.
Catering orders must be placed at least 48 hours in advance.
For catering, we require 50% advance payment at the time of booking.
Catering delivery is available across Islamabad and Rawalpindi (no distance limit).

=== RESERVATIONS ===
Dine-in reservations can be made by calling 051-2345678.
We recommend reservations for groups of 6 or more, especially on weekends.
We hold reservations for 15 minutes past the booked time. After that, the table may be given away.
Private dining room is available for groups of 10-20. Booking requires Rs. 5000 minimum spend.
