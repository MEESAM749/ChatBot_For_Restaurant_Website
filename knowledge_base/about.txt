KARACHI BITES — ABOUT US

=== WHO WE ARE ===
Karachi Bites is a family-owned Pakistani restaurant specializing in authentic Karachi-style street food, BBQ, and karahis. We were founded in 2018 by Chef Hamza Ali, who grew up in Karachi's Burns Garden area and learned cooking from his grandmother.

Our goal is to bring the real taste of Karachi's street food culture to Islamabad. No shortcuts, no frozen ingredients, everything made fresh daily.

=== LOCATION ===
